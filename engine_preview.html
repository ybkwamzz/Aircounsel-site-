What the engine does today

Intake Qualification Engine · Active

This preview focuses on the part of the system that is live, simple, and safe to adopt immediately. No other layer is active. No other layer is part of the initial walkthrough.

The engine applies a structured scoring system to every inbound enquiry before it reaches a solicitor. The result is a qualification report — not a recommendation, not a decision. The solicitor decides. The engine filters the noise.

System Constraints — Layer 1 Only
  • No automation that changes your existing workflow
  • No interference with your case management system
  • No AI-generated legal advice of any kind
  • No privileged client data processed
  • No operational disruption during pilot

The Six Qualification Gates

  1. 01

    Route Fit

    Is the enquiry consistent with a viable UK immigration route for this individual?

  2. 02

    Eligibility Thresholds

    Does the enquiry meet current Home Office salary, qualification, and residency requirements?

  3. 03

    Immigration History

    Are there flags — refusals, overstays, prior applications — that affect viability?

  4. 04

    Fee Capacity

    Can the enquiry realistically proceed as a privately funded matter at your firm's fee level?

  5. 05

    Jurisdiction

    Is this a matter that falls within UK immigration jurisdiction and your firm's remit?

  6. 06

    Urgency

    Is there a time-sensitive element — an expiry, a court date, a removal notice — that affects prioritisation?

Why This Matters to Managers

The problem is not volume.
It is the cost of the wrong volume.

"We don't need more enquiries — we need clarity."

Every non-viable enquiry that reaches a solicitor's desk costs time that cannot be recovered. At a conservative partner rate of £200 per hour, a firm fielding five unqualified enquiries per week is absorbing over £50,000 in lost productivity annually — before a single case is opened.

Layer 1 of the AirCounsel engine gives practice managers a mechanism to protect that time. It does not change how your team works. It changes what reaches them.

The pilot requires no technical setup, no system integration, and no preparation from your staff. You see the output in 10 minutes. You decide whether it works for your firm.

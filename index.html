The Three-Step System

From enquiry to instruction-ready — without a single wasted call

Step 01 · Intake

Structured questions

Clients answer a structured qualification flow — route-specific, compliance-safe, and built on current UK Immigration Rules thresholds. No legal advice. No assumptions.

Step 02 · Filter

Automatic removal

Enquiries scoring below threshold are removed and redirected before they occupy a consultation slot. Scores above 75 trigger a priority callback alert to your team.

Step 03 · Deliver

Only viable cases reach you

A qualification report is delivered to your team: each case scored, ranked, and annotated. Your solicitors start every consultation with full context — not a cold call.
